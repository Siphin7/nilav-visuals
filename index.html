The Artist

Behind the
Lens

I don't arrive at a shoot with a shot list.
I arrive with intention.

Growing up in Kerala, I was surrounded by a world that was effortlessly cinematic — monsoons that painted everything gold, temple festivals ablaze with light, and quiet backwater mornings that felt like they belonged in a film.

That upbringing became the lens through which I see everything. Nilav Visuals isn't a studio — it's a perspective. A way of looking at people, places, and moments that finds the extraordinary inside the ordinary.

Whether I'm shooting a couple before their wedding, a machine that someone loves like family, or a person who just wants the world to see who they really are — my job is to make that truth visible.
